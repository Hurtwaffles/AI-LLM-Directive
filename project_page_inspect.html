LUMEA IPL ↓

Philips Lumea is a device for salon-grade hair removal in the home. Gentle pulses of light to the hair root prevent regrowth, keeping skin smooth for up to 12 months—no razors, no wax, no appointments.

ROLE

Packaging Designer

TYPE

Strategy
Art Direction
Packaging Design
Concepting

Collaborating closely with Philips' product design, app design, and marketing teams, I embarked on a comprehensive design process working in tangent with business to facilitate the release of a medical-grade Lumea product line. With its ergonomic design, smart attachments, and price-tiered accessories, the product landscape was confusing and added friction. Additionally, with development of the Lumea IPL app, we needed to highlight ease of use—letting consumers feel in control with personalized treatment plans and real-time guidance.

The tech was solid, but the experience around it wasn’t. From the shelf to the app, things just felt off—too much noise, too many mixed messages, and not enough clarity where it mattered most. Packaging was overloaded, the app needed better vision, and CRM touchpoints felt like scattered reminders.

By auditing each touchpoint, we were able to bring a cohesive visual story that boosted product familiarity and helped ease inconsistency through the purchase journey.

I invited each team responsible for Lumea to participate in a working session. Together, we mapped user engagement from first consideration to long-term use. For example, by working with the app team, we were able to streamline integration and make sure it was at the forefront of the top-tier lumea offering. We did this by identifying gaps in the CRM flow, packaging design, and consumer journey. By showing where people were dropping off or left in the dark, we helped guide how and where Philips should show up with actual value.

Leading the new packaging design, I stripped away the clutter, clarified product tiers through better layout and visuals, and leaned into a more premium, confident tone. By providing a holistic approach, we ensured the Lumea IPL Series not only met but exceeded user expectations.
